## Verification notes

- **Debezium Postgres prereqs**: Verified `wal_level = logical` is required, `REPLICATION` role attribute is required (not a GRANT), `REPLICA IDENTITY FULL` is required for tables *without* primary keys (the answer frames it as for "full before-images" — technically also correct since FULL writes the entire row to WAL, but the primary trigger is missing PK). [Debezium PG connector docs](https://debezium.io/documentation/reference/stable/connectors/postgresql.html), [Materialize PG-Debezium guide](https://materialize.com/docs/ingest-data/postgres/postgres-debezium/).
- **Spark Structured Streaming + Iceberg MERGE INTO**: Verified the foreachBatch + MERGE INTO pattern is the standard CDC consumer shape; sub-minute latencies are achievable with tuned trigger intervals. The answer's "30-60 seconds" / "1-5 minutes" framing is realistic for a first-cut CDC pipeline. [Iceberg streaming masterclass](https://datalakehousehub.com/blog/2026-04-29-apache-iceberg-masterclass-13-streaming-to-iceberg/), [Debezium + Spark + Iceberg CDC tutorial](https://medium.com/@aalopatin/change-data-capture-cdc-with-debezium-spark-and-iceberg-4472c9a853e2).
